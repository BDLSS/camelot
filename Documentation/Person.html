Person Ontology

http://www.vocab.ox.ac.uk/camelot/Person#

The Person Ontology contains definitions for Person, Adult, Child, Male, Female, Woman, Man, and defines the relationships between them.

Activity

    Role

      Other

      1. AdultA person who is fully grown or developed.

          1. ChildA young human being below the age of puberty or below the legal age of majority.

              1. FemaleThe biological sex that can bear offspring or produce eggs, distinguished biologically by the production of gametes (ova) which can be fertilized by male gametes.

                  1. WomanAn adult human female.

                    • MaleThe biological sex that produces gametes, especially spermatozoa, with which a female may be fertilized or inseminated to produce offspring.

                        1. ManAn adult human male.
