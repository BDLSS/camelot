Activity

  1. AcquaintanceshipThe context in which two people know each other slightly, but who are not close friends

      1. CollaborationActivity of working together to do a task and to achieve shared goals

          1. ContractActivityThe activity that consists of a written or spoken agreement that is intended to be enforceable by law.

              1. DedicationActivityThe activity of addressing or inscribing (a literary work, for example) to another as a mark of respect or affection.

                  1. DedicationOfItemTo address or inscribe an item to another as a mark of respect or affection.

                      1. DedicationOfWorkTo address or inscribe a literary work to another as a mark of respect or affection.

                        • Friendship a relationship of mutual affection between two or more people. Friendship is a stronger form of interpersonal bond than an association.

                            1. InclusionAlbumAmicorumAn activity where a personl includes a creative work in an Album Amicorum

                                1. InterviewA formal meeting in which one or more persons question, consult, or evaluate another person

                                    1. AudienceA formal interview with a person in authority.

                                        1. AudienceWithRulerA formal interview with a ruler i.e. a person exercising government or dominion.

                                        2. IntroductionThe context of one person being introduced to another person by way of a third person who acts as an intermediary.

                                          • {Intermediary} intermediaryOfIntroduction {Introduction}

                                          1. JourneyThe activity of travelling from one place to another. Travel is the movement of people between relatively distant geographical locations, and can involve travel by foot, bicycle, automobile, train, boat, airplane, or other means, with or without luggage, and can be one way or round trip. Travel can also include relatively short stays between successive movements.

                                            • {Journey} hasArrivalDate
                                            • {Journey} hasDepartureDate

                                            1. MeetingActivity when two or more people meet, by chance or arrangement

                                                1. SocialGatheringA gathering for the purpose of promoting fellowship

                                                    1. SalonA regular social gathering, especially of writers and artists

                                                      • SocialVisitThe activity of going to see and spend time with (someone) socially

                                                        • TradeTrade is to transfer the ownership of goods from one person or entity to another by getting something in exchange from the buyer.

                                                            1. Transmissionthe action or process of transmitting something or the state of being transmitted

                                                                1. Correspondencean activity where one person writes a letter to another person.

                                                                    1. GiftGivingThis context describes the activity of giving and receiving a gift.

                                                                        1. LiteraryExchangeAn activity that involves the exchange between two people of a literary creative work, e.g. a book.

                                                                            1. ExchangeOccasionalVerseAn activity where one person authors verse and sends it to another person.
