Activity

  1. DisplacementThe enforced departure of people from their homes, typically because of war, persecution, or natural disaster.

      1. EmmigrationThe activity of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another; moving abroad.

          1. ExileThe state of being barred from one's native country, typically for political or punitive reasons.

              1. ImmigrationThe activity of of coming to live permanently in a foreign country.

                  1. ResidenceThe activity of living in a particular place.

                      1. TravelThe activity of making a journey, typically of some length.

                          1. VisitThe activity of going to see a person or place as a guest, tourist, etc.
