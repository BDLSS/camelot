Learned Activity Ontology

Activity

  1. AuthoringActivityActivity that involves the writing of a book, article or document.

      1. ContributionActivityActivity that involves the contiribution of something by a person or thing

          1. CreationActivityActivity that brings something into existence.

              1. DedicationActivityAn activity that involves an address or inscription (of a literary work, for example) to another as a mark of respect or affection

                  1. EditingActivityActivity of preparing written material for publication by correcting, condensing, or otherwise modifying it.

                      1. PrintingActivityActivity that involves the production of books, newspapers or other printed materials.

                          1. PublishingActivityThe activity of preparing and issuing books, journals and other materials for sale.

                              1. ResearchAn activity involving the systematic investigation into and study of materials and sources in order to establish facts and reach new conclusions.

                                  1. ReviewActivityAn activity involving the formal assessment of something with the intention of instituting change if necessary

                                      1. TranslationActivityAn activity involving the translation of words or text from one language into another.
